Co-relations between the rates of deforestization and carbon absorption

How does it work ?

Total Forest Area

Displays the percent of the total area that the forest of the specified country covers.

Deforestation Rate

Displays the percentage of the countries forest that has been deforested and cut down.

CO2 Absorption Rate

Lack of carbon aborption over the period of deforestization relative to the country.

Trees can't keep up

The Issue at Hand

Currently in our society we do not see first hand that the rates of deforestation of the tropical rainforest are at play in a negative way. We are loosing alot of carbon sequentration through the absorption of the trees. Throughout a fifteen year span from 1990 to 2005, Sundered Lumber

displays the ratio and relationships of how big the different areas of the forest are compared to how much of them have been cut down.
